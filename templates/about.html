About Us

Maxrides is an innovative online vehicle leasing services for E-hailing and delivery professionals in South Africa on platforms such as; Indrive, Uber, Bolt, UberEats, Takealot, etc. We pride ourselves in offering a unique service that connects our registered drivers to vehicle owners, car dealerships, and van/truck rental companies in South Africa. We provide easy access to vehicles at an affordable price to individuals that do not have a car to start or to continue their E-hailing or delivery driving business/job.
Our partnerships with various vehicle dealerships, van/ mini-truck rental companies, and private car owners have enabled us to provide our rental and hire purchase services to over 2000 E-hailing and delivery professionals in over 15 cities in South Africa.
We have our agents and partner vehicle providers in all locations where our services are currently offered and we continuously strive to provide our services to our registered customers as quickly as possible.
