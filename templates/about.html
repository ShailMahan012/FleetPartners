About Us

MaxRide is an innovative and unique car rental platform tailor-made for individuals that need a vehicle to use as a driver on ride-hailing platforms in Nigeria such as Bolt, Uber, Indrive, Rider etc. We pride ourself in offering a unique service that provides access to vehicles at an affordable price to individuals that do not have a car to start or to continue their ride-hailing driving business. Our several partnerships with various car dealerships and individual private car owners in Nigeria has enabled us to provide our car rental and hire purchase services to our ever growing number of customers in over 24 states in Nigeria. We have agents located in all 24 states where our services are currently offered and we continuously strive to provide our car rental services to our customers as quickly as possible.
